System Overview

A Professional Performance Suite.

SafeBeat is a high-fidelity cardiac monitoring ecosystem designed to provide real-time electrical heart data. Unlike standard monitors, SafeBeat captures the full ECG waveform, providing a clinical-grade window into an athlete's cardiovascular health.

Cross-Platform Sync

Seamless data flow between the wearable hardware, mobile application, and this professional web command center.

The Crisis

Silence is the Danger.

Sudden Cardiac Arrest (SCA) often strikes without warning. Standard fitness trackers fail to detect the underlying electrical irregularities that precede a crisis. SafeBeat addresses this by analyzing the P-QRS-T complex in real-time, identifying risks before they manifest physically.

The Prototype

Invisible Engineering.

Our solution integrates the AD8232 heart monitor frontend with dual-core ESP32 processing nodes. This hardware is housed in an impact-resistant, lightweight module designed to clip onto high-performance compression apparel.

By utilizing dry-contact electrodes, the system maintains a stable signal even during high-intensity explosive movements.
